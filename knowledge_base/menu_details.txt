=== CONE TIPS FROM THE PROS ===

First time building? Here are our tips:

- Start simple: Pick 1 or 2 blends max for your first cone
- Our cinnamon sugar cone pairs perfectly with almost any combination
- Don't skip the drizzle - it's only $0.75 and takes it to the next level!
- Premium blends (Brownie, Strawberry, Blueberry, Mango, Raspberry, and Pineapple) are worth the upgrade
- Mix textures: combine crunchy (pretzels, graham cracker) with creamy (cookie dough blend, Nutella filling, Cookie Butter filling)
- Can't decide? Ask our team for their favorite combo!

=== POPULAR QUESTIONS ===

Q: Can I get multiple blends?
A: Absolutely! Every Cone N' Swirl and Cup N' Swirl comes with one traditional blend. You can also add one of our Premium Blends for a $1.25 each or add an extra traditional blend or more for $0.75 each

Q: What's the difference between a blend and a stick'em?
A: Blends are blended INTO the ice cream. Stick'ems are stuck on top of your ice cream as a non-blended item.

Q: Can I get half and half ice cream?
A: Unfortunately not. Our auger blender is very powerful and your half and half ice cream will come out looking like a light colored chocolate mix.

Q: Do you have sugar-free options?
A: We do not. Please feel free to ask one of our team members for a few suggestions when ordering to best match your palate.

Q: How messy will this get?
A: As with most ice cream treats, it does melt and you may get messy. With every Cone N' Swirl or Cone Only (No Ice Cream) order we not only offer a napkin but a towelette to leave you with a clean Cone N' Swirl experience.
