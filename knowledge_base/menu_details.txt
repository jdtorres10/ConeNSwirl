=== CONE TIPS FROM THE PROS ===

First time building? Here are our tips:

- Start simple: Pick 1 or 2 blends max for your first cone
- Our cinnamon sugar cone pairs perfectly with almost any combination
- Chocolate base + Nutella filling = dessert heaven
- Don't skip the drizzle - it's only $0.50 and takes it to the next level!
- Premium blends (Brownie, Strawberry, Blueberry, Mango) are worth the upgrade
- Mix textures: combine crunchy (pretzels, graham cracker) with creamy (cookie dough, Nutella)
- Can't decide? Ask our team for their favorite combo!

=== POPULAR QUESTIONS ===

Q: Can I get multiple blends?
A: Absolutely! First blend is included, additional blends are +$1.00 each (standard) or +$1.50 (premium)

Q: What's the difference between a blend and a stick'em?
A: Blends are mixed INTO the ice cream. Stick'ems are stuck to the OUTSIDE of your cone for extra crunch!

Q: Can I get half and half ice cream?
A: Yes! We can swirl vanilla and chocolate together.

Q: Do you have sugar-free options?
A: Not at this time, but we're always exploring new options based on customer feedback.

Q: How messy will this get?
A: Pretty messy! We give plenty of napkins. Pro tip: eat the stick'ems first before they fall off!

Q: Can I sample flavors?
A: Our ice cream bases are vanilla and chocolate, but you can ask for a small taste of any blend before committing!
