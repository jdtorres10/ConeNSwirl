FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

=== ORDERING & PAYMENT ===

Q: How do I place an order?
A: Walk up to our window and order in person. We are always available if you have any questions. We are more than happy to help guide you through your Cone N' Swirl experience.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), Apple Pay, and Google Pay

Q: Is there a minimum purchase?
A: No minimum! Order as little or as much as you'd like. Our menu is designed so that each order is uniquely yours. 

Q: Do you offer discounts?
A: Yes! Follow us on social media for flash deals and promotions. Ask the team members about any discounts that you may be eligible for. 

Q: Can I call ahead to pre-order?
A: Not at this time our creamy soft serve like ice cream melts, but we're fast! We take pride in keeping your wait short! 

=== MENU & DIETARY ===
Q: Do you have allergen information?
A: Yes! Just ask at the window. Common allergens: our kitchen uses dairy, wheat, soy, and tree nuts. We can't guarantee zero cross-contamination but will do our best to accommodate. At this time, while on the trailer, if you have a tree nut allergy we will mix in your blends with a spatula. When we open our storefront we plan on having two blenders in operation, with one being specific for tree nut allergy customers to give you the Cone N' Swirl experience as well. 

Q: Can I customize my order?
A: Yes! Every order is unique, that's what makes Cone N' Swirl special, your options are endless! You can make it as crazy as you'd like or as simple. Each order is perfectly yours.

Q: Do you have a kids menu?
A: While we don't have a separate kids menu, our Cup N' Swirls make a great size for little tummys.

=== GENERAL ===

Q: How long is the typical wait?
A: Usually 5-10 minutes during lunch rush. Approximately 1-3 minutes during slower times. We work quickly!

Q: Is there seating?
A: Not usually, it varies by location. Most customers enjoy their treats nearby. 

Q: Do you have napkins, utensils, etc.?
A: Yes! Everything you need is available at our window - napkins and utensils. We also offer small towelettes for every Cone N' Swirl order as we understand ice cream can get a bit messy as it melts.  

Q: How can I stay updated on new menu items?
A: Follow us on our socials Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook! We announce specials, seasonal items, and menu changes there.

Q: Are you hiring?
A: Email Subject Jobs conenswirl@gmail.com with your resume and availability. We're always looking for friendly, personable, and hardworking team members.

Q: What makes your food special?
A: One evening, we stumbled upon a show featuring these incredible Hungarian chimney cones. We were just curious to try them as guests—but when we looked around San Antonio and Central Texas, there were none. We drove all the way to Houston just to taste one! After that trip, we were hooked. We spent two months experimenting in our kitchen, testing countless recipes with friends and neighbors until we perfected our own.

What truly sets us apart is the fusion we created. Instead of traditional soft-serve, we offer a blended ice cream experience—similar to New Zealand-style soft serve, but with our own twist. We start with scooped ice cream, and aside from real fruit as an option, we can blend in coffee, almonds, pecans, Oreos, brownies, and much more.

But that's not all—each swirl is your creation. Our menu is fully customizable, giving you the freedom to craft your perfect cone or cup. At Cone N' Swirl, every swirl is uniquely yours. We can't wait to share this journey—and a swirl—with you!
